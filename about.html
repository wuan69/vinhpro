The Journey

My basketball journey started on the concrete courts, driven by an obsession with the mechanics of the game. After a successful collegiate career, I transitioned into professional play overseas, where I learned the nuances of European ball movement and high-level defensive schemes.

However, my true calling was always teaching. I realized that pure athleticism only takes you so far; the separator at the HAPPY level is Basketball IQ and Mastery of Fundamentals.

For the past 8 years, I've dedicated myself to breaking down the game for players at all levels. My philosophy is simple: Train at game speed, build unbreakable habits, and learn how to read the floor.

Coaching Philosophy

"We don't do drills just to get tired. Every movement, every rep must simulate a game situation. Mental conditioning is just as important as physical reps."

Teaching Style

Intense, detail-oriented, and highly analytical. I use video breakdown to correct micro-mechanics in your shot and footwork.
